Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 19, 2025

MinimaLog ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address, name, and authentication credentials (via Google Sign-In or Apple Sign-In).
  • Workout Data: Information about your workouts, exercises, sets, reps, weights, and personal records.
  • Profile Information: Optional information such as profile picture, username, bio, and weight unit preferences.
  • User-Generated Content: Posts, comments, and other content you create within the app.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: App features used, workout completion times, and app interactions.
  • Network Information: Connection status for offline/online sync features.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing and maintaining the app's core functionality
  • Syncing your workout data across devices
  • Generating workout statistics and progress tracking
  • Personalizing your experience with custom templates and recommendations
  • Processing in-app purchases and subscription management
  • Sending important notifications about your workouts and progress
  • Improving app performance and fixing bugs
  • Complying with legal obligations

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Where Your Data is Stored

  • Cloud Storage: Your workout data is stored securely using Supabase (PostgreSQL database) with encryption in transit and at rest.
  • Local Storage: The app stores encrypted data locally on your device for offline functionality.
  • Images: Exercise images and profile pictures are stored using secure cloud storage.

3.2 Security Measures

  • All data transmission uses HTTPS/SSL encryption
  • Sensitive data is encrypted using AES-GCM encryption
  • Authentication tokens are securely stored and encrypted
  • Row-level security policies protect your data from unauthorized access

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

4.1 Service Providers

  • Supabase: Database and authentication services
  • RevenueCat: Subscription and payment processing
  • Google AI (Gemini): Optional AI-powered workout tips (only if you use this feature)

4.2 Social Features

  • Posts you create may be visible to other users (if you choose to share them)
  • Your profile information may be visible to users who follow you

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes or government requests
  • Protect our rights, property, or safety
  • Prevent fraud or security issues

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all your personal data within the app
  • Update: Edit your profile and workout data at any time
  • Delete: Delete individual workouts, posts, or your entire account
  • Export: Download your workout data (coming soon)

5.2 Account Deletion

To delete your account:

  1. Open the app and go to Profile → Settings
  2. Scroll to the bottom and tap "Delete Account"
  3. Confirm deletion

Note: Account deletion is permanent and will delete all your workout data, posts, and profile information.

6. Children's Privacy

MinimaLog is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and maintained on servers located outside of your country. By using the app, you consent to the transfer of information to countries outside of your country of residence, which may have different data protection rules.

8. Third-Party Services

The app integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In: For authentication (Privacy Policy)
  • Apple Sign-In: For authentication (Privacy Policy)
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management (Privacy Policy)
  • Supabase: For data storage (Privacy Policy)

9. Cookies and Tracking

The app uses local storage and cookies for:

  • Authentication and session management
  • Offline data caching
  • User preferences (theme, unit settings)

We do not use third-party tracking or advertising cookies.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Sending you a notification (for material changes)

11. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal information within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal compliance.

12. Your California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect and how we use it
  • Delete your personal information
  • Opt-out of the sale of your personal information (we do not sell your data)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

13. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

Email: support@minimalog.fit
